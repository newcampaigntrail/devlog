On The Deleting of Achievement Data

2023-05-14

Recently, thanks to the change from newcampaigntrail.github.io to newcampaigntrail.com, the local storage keeping themes and achievements on NCT has been reset.

In general, we cannot help recover this. This was an oversight on my [Decstar's] part, and the other devs shouldn't be blamed for this issue. It didn't even occur to me that this would occur during the domain shift.

If you desperately need certain achievements back, you can contact a dev, and we can have a look into what we can do (in the case of extremely hard-to-get achievements, such as the JFK or 2016a one).

I would like to offer my deepest apologies for the inconvenience.
