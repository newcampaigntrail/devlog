Roadmap To Multiplayer

2023-05-15

Multiplayer NCT is something that has been on the community's mind for a long time. Recently, we've been working to refine a coherent model for NCT multiplayer going into the future.

If you are interested, help and input from modmakers would be appreciated in this design process, specifically in regards to designing multiplayer-specific mods.

There is more to come on this in the coming months.
